Cigarette soup

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 1-12

Ingredients Quantity
Cigarettes 500
Water 1 liter per 100 cigarettes
  • Step 1: Pour water into a stewpot.
  • Step 2: Add 500 cigarettes.
  • Step 3: Cook until boiling point.

Nauryz kozhe

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 1-6

Ingredients Quantity
Meat 500 g
Water Enough to boil meat
Airan 1 l
Salt To taste
Rice 50 g
Barley 100 g
Noodles 50 g
  • Step 1: Boil your meat products in sufficient amount of water (to cover the meat in the pot) for at least 2-3 hours. Readiness depends on the type of meat. Make sure to skim off foam that builds up when it just starts boiling. That gives you clear stock. When ready take out the meat.
  • Step 2: Have your barley half cooked by boiling it in salted (reasonably) water while the meat is being cooked. Then when the meat is ready pop it in together with rice.
  • Step 3: Add the noodles after rice and barley been in stock for 10-15 minutes. Give it 5 more minutes for the noodles to be ready. Best is to use home-made noodles. Some don’t like noodles in soup. So if you are one of them and don’t like it neither then just miss it out.
  • Step 4: Slice the meat up in smaller chunks and add it back to the pot.
  • Step 5: Let it all cool down a bit before you add the kefir. And voila. Enjoy your meal. Wait, did I tell you that it's nice cold.

Chleb z cementem

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 14

Ingredients Quantity
Wheat bread 1 loaf
Water 700 ml
Sand 300 g
Scree 450 g
M400 cement 100 g
  • Step 1: Slice the loaf.
  • Step 2: Mix the cement and medley it until it reaches a homogeneous condition.
  • Step 3: Spread the cement on the bread.
